About

This website serves as a dedicated platform for WMSU students, faculty, and alumni seeking freelance opportunities. It features a marketplace where freelancers can offer their services and connect with potential clients. The platform includes a booking system for easy scheduling and a chat system to facilitate seamless communication. Our mission is to empower the WMSU community by providing a space to showcase skills, find jobs, and build professional networks.

Specialized per College

Freelancers are categorized by their college at WMSU, helping clients easily find talents specialized in different fields.

Freelance Marketplace

This platform connects WMSU students, faculty, and alumni with freelance job opportunities, offering services across various industries.

Booking System

Freelancers can set their availability, allowing clients to conveniently book services based on schedules that work best for both.

Real-Time Chat

The platform features a built-in chat system, enabling direct and real-time communication between freelancers and clients.

Secure Transactions

The website ensures a safe and reliable environment for managing bookings, communications, and transactions within the WMSU community.
